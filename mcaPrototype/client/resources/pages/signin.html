Help! How do I make an account/sign in?

Creating an account:

  1. Enter your email address
  2. Choose a password at least 8 characters long
  3. Click "sign up" to create your account

Logging in:

  1. Enter your email address
  2. Enter the password you chose upon creating your account
  3. Click sign in!

If you forgot your password, you can click the "forgot password" button, which will prompt you to enter the email you used to sign up for your account
